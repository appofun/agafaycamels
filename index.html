About Us

About Agafay Camels

Agafay Camels offers memorable camel ride experiences in the rocky Agafay Desert near Marrakech. Whether you want a peaceful sunset ride, a romantic desert dinner, a family-friendly activity, or a complete camel ride and quad bike combo, Agafay is one of the easiest ways to enjoy a true desert atmosphere close to Marrakech.

The Agafay Desert landscape offers unique light, warm desert colors, dramatic rocky scenery, and beautiful evening views that make it a favorite destination for travelers looking for an authentic Moroccan desert experience without the long journey to the Sahara.

Practical Advice

Agafay Camel Ride Tips

Make the most of your Agafay Desert experience with these practical travel tips.

01

Wear comfortable, loose clothes suitable for a camel ride in warm desert conditions.

02

Bring sunglasses — the desert light and glare can be strong, especially in the afternoon.

03

Bring a light jacket for the evening — Agafay gets cool after sunset, especially outside summer.

04

Charge your phone fully before the trip — sunset camel photos are unforgettable and you'll want storage.

05

Ask about dinner and entertainment options before booking to plan your budget and timing clearly.

06

Confirm your pickup location and time clearly — hotels, riads, or central meeting points all work.

07

Choose a private transfer if you want more comfort, flexibility, and personalized service.

08

Respect the animals, local staff, and the desert environment throughout your experience.
